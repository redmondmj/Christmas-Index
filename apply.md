**Deadline: December 10, 2025**

To apply for support, please complete the application form. You can submit it via email to [christmasindex@bellaliant.com](mailto:christmasindex@bellaliant.com).

### How to get an application:
1. **Download:** [Client Application PDF](/assets/pdf/ChristmasHolidayIndexApplication.pdf)
2. **Pick up in person:** Forms are available at:

   * Food Network
   * Maggies Place
   * Salvation Army Thrift Store
   * Community Services office
